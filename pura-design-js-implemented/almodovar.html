Almodovar

We have designed interiors inspired by the colors of Pedro Almodovar's movies. Main colors are saturated carmine red, honey yellow and shades of blue, which are characteristic of the director's work. Vibrant colors combined with lush, green plants create a warm, Spanish atmosphere of the place.
